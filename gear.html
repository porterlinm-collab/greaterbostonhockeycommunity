Hockey Gear

Resources for finding (or finding a home for) hockey gear.

I'm new to hockey! What gear do I need?

Almost all hockey programming will require you to have a full set of hockey gear. Here's the list:

  • Helmet (w/ cage or shield)
  • Shoulder pads
  • Elbow pads
  • Hockey pants
  • Protective cup (jill)
  • Shin pads
  • Hockey socks (goes over shin pads)
  • Gloves
  • Skates (hockey skates!)
  • Stick (make sure you're getting an ICE hockey stick)
  • Neck guard (optional, but strongly recommended)
  • Jersey
  • Mouthguard (optional, but strongly recommended)
