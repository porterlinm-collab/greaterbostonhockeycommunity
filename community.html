Belmont Women's Hockey League Announces First Annual Women's Hockey Camp

The Belmont Women's Hockey League is introducing its first annual Women's Hockey Camp, running the weekend of August 28th–30th. The camp will feature both on-ice and off-ice sessions, with all-women coaches. More information coming soon — registration is now open!

3v3 Pond Hockey Returns This Summer

Last summer, the Belmont Women's Hockey League held their first-ever pond hockey-style league, in a 3v3 format. This year, the BWHL brings back this unique program, which will run for 10 weeks at the brand new Vigliroloi Rink in Belmont! The league will roster 4 teams of 8, and games will be cross-ice.

StinkySocks Hosts Women's Division in Boston's Best Tournament

Four women's teams competed this year in the newly introduced women's division of the annual Boston's Best Tournament, held May 1st–3rd. Games were played at Cronin Rink in Revere and Quincy Youth Arena. The Mother Puckers and the Stick Figures faced off in the final championship game, with Stick Figures taking home the trophy. Congrats to the Stick Figures!
